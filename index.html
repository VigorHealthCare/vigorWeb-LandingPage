The Challenge

Bridging the Accountability Gap

Sending money home for healthcare shouldn't be a gamble. We understand the emotional worry and the lack of transparency in the current process.

💸

Misallocated Funds

Money meant for health bills often gets used for other family needs, leaving loved ones without proper care.

📉

Zero Visibility

Diaspora sponsors often have no way to verify if their loved ones actually saw a doctor or received treatment.

😰

Emotional Stress

The constant worry of "are they actually okay?" shouldn't be part of your daily life abroad.

Who We Are

Our Mission

VigorCare was founded to bridge the gap between Nigerian families and their loved ones abroad. We believe that distance shouldn't mean a lack of quality care or transparency.

We are a team of healthcare professionals and technology enthusiasts dedicated to improving the primary healthcare system in Nigeria. By providing a secure, EMR-driven platform, we ensure that every kobo sent for healthcare is accounted for and results in professional medical attention.

Verified Providers

Our Healthcare Network

We partner with verified Primary Healthcare Centers (PHCs) and professional medical specialists across Nigeria.

Verified Clinics

Over 50+ partner clinics across Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt, all integrated with our EMR system.

Certified Specialists

Access to a network of certified doctors, nurses, and specialists for video consultations and in-person care.
